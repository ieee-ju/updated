About the Event

REWIRED v1.0 aims to allow you to witness the true computing power of electronics. We have already seen a world grow in the domains of Software and Computing. We've even seen entire cities build upon this theme, "IT".

The truth is however, it has overshadowed the reality of the industry. To run the newest and greatest applications, you will be requiring a place to run it on. REWIRED v1.0 will be the starting point for you in this domain. While learning software is a crucial part of the game, learning how to design your own hardware will allow you to take it to the next level.

Through a thoroughly paced workshop aiming at teaching you the basics of circuit design, we take it a step further where you build your ideas, into real physical devices. The beauty of hardware is you can see it come to life, right in front of your eyes.

Not only will learning how to build Printed Circuit Boards give you an edge above everyone, but it will be an invaluable skills in all technical fests, projects and events.
